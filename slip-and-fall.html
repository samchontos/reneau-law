Slip & Fall

A fall can cause fractures, head injuries, and lasting disability. When a hazard should have been fixed or flagged, the property owner may owe you compensation.

How We Help

Slip & Fall claims in Georgia

Slip-and-fall and trip-and-fall claims turn on notice: did the property owner know, or should they have known, about the dangerous condition, and did they fail to fix it or warn about it in time?

Attorney Reneau moves quickly to secure the evidence that decides these cases — surveillance video, cleaning and inspection logs, and witness statements — before it disappears, and pursues fair compensation for injuries that are often more serious than they first appear.

What these cases often involve

  • 01Wet, uneven, or obstructed floors and walkways
  • 02Missing handrails, poor lighting, and hidden hazards
  • 03Ice, spills, and debris left unaddressed
  • 04Prompt preservation of video and maintenance records
